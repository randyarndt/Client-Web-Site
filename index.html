Watch Repair

A modern Swiss watch “beats” 691,200 times per day as the teeth of the escape wheel strike the jewels of the pallet lever. The consistency of this motion determines the accuracy of your watch. Therefore it is a good idea to get your timepiece serviced regularly. It is a privilege for Mangata & Gallo Jewelers to be a Rolex Authorized Service Center. We therefore belong to the world-wide community of watchmakers educated by Rolex who have been hand-picked for their undeniable skill and reliability.
